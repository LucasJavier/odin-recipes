Lasagna

This classic cuisine is famous all over the world, not only in Italy. Ideal to wear on the carpet to work or school, and it certainly triumphs among the little ones at home. Do not overdo the amount, with a portion is enough, a fairly complete recipe and gives you to overcome a day full of energy.a

What I always recommend is to use quality ingredients, from pasta, in this case lasagna Garofalo, one of the best pasta in Italy. And of course, the fillings, today meat, but you have many ways to fill it. Following the recipe you can make lasagna with vegetables, the classic lasagna with tuna or lasagna with fish, which is a great option, tuna, hake, even combine it with seafood.

Ingredients

For the filling (meat bolognese):

  • 500 g. meat (a mixture of pork and veal)
  • 2 red peppers
  • 2 carrots
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 150 g. bacon or bacon
  • 250 g. natural tomato (about 1 glass)
  • 250 ml white wine (200 ml. approximately)
  • 100 ml. extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 heaping teaspoon of dried oregano (or Provencal herbs)
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper (to taste each house)

For the lasagna:

  • 12 sheets of lasagna or lasagna Garofalo

To make the bechamel: (for a liter or less, enough for lasagna):

  • 125 g of all-purpose wheat flour
  • 125 g of butter
  • 1 liter of whole milk
  • A pinch of nutmeg (about 4 g.)
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper (to taste each house)

To finish and gratinate the lasagna:

  • 100-120 g. grated cheese Grana Padano (or mix with your favorite cheese to gratin)

Steps

Preparation of the meat lasagna

  1. Heat a large pot of water, the widest at home. When it begins to boil, we pour 2 generous handfuls of salt.
  2. We introduce the lasagna sheets one by one without touching them (so that they do not stick together). Now we can find countless types of lasagna where it does not hydrate as it was done before. At home many times to save time I use those that are hydrated with the bechamel and the juice that drops the sauce when baking.
  3. If you do it in the traditional way we have to stir with a wooden spoon and in about 10 minutes we remove the sheets. We stretch them on top of some sheets of absorbent kitchen paper. Even if you think they are not, they will end up in the oven.
  4. The next step will be to wash very well all the vegetables that we will use in the filling. In the recipe I recommend the stuffing of the classic bolognese sauce, carrots, garlic, peppers and onion.

How to make meat lasagna filling

  1. Cut the vegetables into small pieces so that they are well combined in the sauce. The carrots are cut as thin as possible. I recommend to laminate them with the peeler of the potatoes because at the time of pocharlas if the strips are thick they are not made. We reserve everything in a bowl.
  2. In another casserole we pour extra virgin olive oil. Start by poaching the onion and garlic, when golden, add the rest of the ingredients.
  3. We fry everything at average temperature within about 15 minutes and we wait for meat. We pepper meat to taste and we stack it on a casserole with greens. Let it pass for 5 minutes and when we see it changing color introduce bacon or bacon in very small pieces. Pour a glass of white wine and wait for it to reduce (another 5 minutes on medium heat).
  4. Add a glass of natural tomato, pour the teaspoon of oregano and rectify salt and pepper if necessary (try the salsa to see if it is to your liking). Stir the meat with the vegetables and remove from the heat, let cool a little.
  5. Preheat the oven to 200ºC for 15 minutes, just enough to make the rest of the lasagna.

Preparation of the bechamel sauce and end of the recipe

  1. While the sauce rests we are going to prepare the bechamel, we can prepare it creamier or more consistent depending on the measures. In the blog you have the recipe of this cream with all the details.
  2. To assemble the lasagna the thing is very simple. We put at the bottom of the fountain a few tablespoons of the béchamel. Top the sheets, a layer of the mixture with the meat in each sheet, again the béchamel sauce and so on until 3 floors.
  3. You can make as many layers of lasagna as you want, as the source allows. Finally, we finish with a generous layer of béchamel sauce. And for those who like cheese, let's grate the one you like, which is special for grating. I recommend a Grana Padano curadito cheese.
  4. Bake on the middle tray for 15 minutes at 180º C and for 3-5 minutes on top with the gratin put so that it browns. Ready to eat, no need for rest, from oven to table.
  5. This recipe besides being economical is a healthy recipe, full of proteins and carbohydrates. I always present it as a unique dish, I hope you like it.
